Welcome to Zero 2 Hero.

Zero 2 Hero is a score between 0 and 100 generated each day based on a combination of your current autonomic response, training status and intrinsic motivation, where 0 would indicate not able to train at all and 100 being you are at your peak for the World Championships.

Your score does not change during the day, so undertaking strenuous or stressful activity prior to your planned training will impact the reliability of your score.

First step is to authorise your Today's Plan account.
